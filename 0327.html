How can I do tag wrapping in Visual Studio Code?

Embedded Emmet could do the trick:

  • Select text (optional)
  • Open command palette (Press F1)
  • Execute Emmet: Wrap with Abbreviation
  • Enter a tag p or h1 (or an abbreviation .wrapper>p)
  • Hit Enter
